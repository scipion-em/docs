Contact Us¶

Mailing list¶

The easiest way to reach us is by writing to our mailing list: scipion-users@lists.sourceforge.net You can also subscribe to the list here, to get all the notifications.

Email¶

If your reason to contact us is not suitable to share with all users, then reach us at scipion@cnb.csic.es.

Create Issues in GitHub¶

If you have an account on GitHub (It is very quick to create a new one), you can Open a New Issue to report a bug or suggest a new feature

Join us on Slack¶

We have a slack channel where our most active members keep in touch daily. You can request access on scipion@cnb.csic.es .
