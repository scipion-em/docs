9. Morph the predicted model onto the docked fragments and rebuild the missing parts of the model in the map density¶

In this section of the AlphaFold tutorial we are going to rebuild the docked TACAN monomer structure finally obtained in the previous section.

Fig. 9.23 Part of the general Scipion workflow performed to rebuild the AlphaFold2 prediction structure of the human TACAN complex.¶

We are going to take advantage of the protocol phenix-rebuild predicted model to rebuild the docked structure prediction into the map (protocol appendix). As the title of this section indicates, the rebuild function morph the predicted model onto the docked fragments and rebuild the missing parts of the model in the map density. The result, however, depends very much on the map we are working with. In any case, the result anyway can still be improved by additional rounds of processing.
