2. Atomic Structure Chain Operator protocol¶

Protocol designed to perform two types of operations with chains from atomic structures in : a) Chain extraction: An individual chain will be extracted from a polymeric atomic structure. The extracted chain will be saved as monomer in a new atomic structure and will not include HETATM and water molecules. b) Chain addition: One or several chains will be added to a reference atomic structure. The resulting addition will be saved as a new polymeric atomic structure.
